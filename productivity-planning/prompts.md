# Productivity & Planning Prompts

## Plan Your Convention Schedule
```
I'm a professional tattoo artist attending [convention name] in [city] from [dates].
I have [number] days there. Help me create a day-by-day schedule that balances:
- Time at my booth tattooing clients
- Networking with other artists
- Attending panels or competitions
- Rest and meals
- Any sightseeing or prep I should factor in
My priority is: [e.g. getting bookings / networking / competing]
```

## Weekly Planning for a Traveling Artist
```
I'm a tattoo artist based in Thailand who travels frequently to Europe for conventions and guest spots.
This week I'm in [location]. Help me plan my week with these tasks to complete:
[paste your to-do list]
Prioritize by urgency and group similar tasks together. Factor in time zones — I need to communicate with clients in [locations].
```

## Convention Prep Checklist
```
I'm preparing to travel to [convention name] in [city, country] as a [guest artist / competitor / judge].
I leave in [X] days. Create a comprehensive checklist covering:
- Equipment to pack
- Documents and paperwork needed
- Studio/booth setup
- Client bookings to confirm
- Social media to prepare
- Travel logistics
```

## Life Strategy & Goal Setting
```
I'm a professional tattoo artist and [other roles]. My goals for the next 6 months are:
[list your goals]
Help me break these down into monthly milestones, weekly actions, and daily habits.
Be realistic about the fact that I travel frequently and have inconsistent schedules.
```

## Beat Procrastination on Admin Tasks
```
I'm an artist who finds admin tasks draining. I need to complete:
[list tasks]
Help me tackle these using the Pomodoro technique — give me a structured session plan with
25-minute work blocks and 5-minute breaks. Make the tasks feel manageable and give me
a motivating reason to start right now.
```
