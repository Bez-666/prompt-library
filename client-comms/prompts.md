# Client Communication Prompts

## Reply to a Booking Enquiry
```
I'm a tattoo artist. A potential client sent me this message: "[paste their message]"

Write a professional, friendly reply that:
- Thanks them for reaching out
- Asks the key questions I need (design details, placement, size, budget, timeline)
- Reflects my style as an experienced artist
- Keeps it concise
```

## Explain Your Pricing
```
Help me write a short, friendly explanation of why custom tattoo pricing starts at [your minimum] per hour / per piece. 
The client seems hesitant about cost. Emphasize quality, experience, and the permanence of tattoos without being defensive.
```

## Handle a Difficult Client Request
```
A client is asking me to copy another artist's tattoo design exactly. 
Help me write a polite but firm response explaining why I don't do direct copies, and offer an alternative approach that keeps them interested in booking with me.
```

## Post-Tattoo Aftercare Instructions
```
Write clear, friendly aftercare instructions for a fresh tattoo. 
Cover: first 24 hours, washing, moisturising, sun exposure, swimming, and signs of infection to watch for.
Make it easy to understand for someone who has never had a tattoo before.
```
